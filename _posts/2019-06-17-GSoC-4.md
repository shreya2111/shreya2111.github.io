                * …
            - json/
                * meta.json

                        {sample_name: <str>,
                            ground_truth: <transcript string>}, …}
                * [sample_name].words.json
                        [{text: <str>,
                          start_time: <float>,
                          end_time: <float> }, …]
                * [sample_name].phonemes.json
                        [{text: <str>,
                          start_time: <float>,
